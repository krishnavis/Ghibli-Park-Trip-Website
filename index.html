About the Event

The works of Studio Ghibli have moved hearts and stirred emotions throughout the world. From the beautiful animation to the complex plot lines, the art Studio Ghibli has been highly regarded. For this reason, a trip to visit Ghibli Park in Japan will be held in early August where lovers of Ghibli are invited to attend for a group outing.
